CodeFury 3.0

IEEE UVCE Student Branch is proud to present IEEE UVCE Computer Society's Annual National level Hackathon CodeFury 3.0 which is scheduled to take place on the 7th and 8th of November, 2020 . Our vision is to conduct Hackathons every year to help solve some of the pivotal problems everyone, as a technical society, face in day to day activities and to have a positive impact on the society at large.

"CodeFury 3.0" is a National level Hackathon which will draw participants from all over India. The main task of the Hackathon is to create a Web or Android Application related to the given problem domain. The problem domain would be given on the spot.

requirements.txt

  • The participants must be below 23 years of age.
  • The team can consist of a maximum of 4 members and minimun of 2 members.
  • The participants will have to be currently pursuing an under-graduation degree in any college and must hold a valid College ID for the same.

Licences.txt

  • You agree that the details provided in the registration form are true and complete to the best of your knowledge. The CodeFury 3.0 organising committee is in no way responsible for any incorrect information provided by the participants.
  • The registration fees, once paid, cannot be refunded under any circumstances.
  • Any violation of the "Requirement.txt" will lead to immediate disqualification of the team.
  • The participants will have to design the webpage/android app using any of the following IDEs – Android Studio, Netbeans, Eclipse, Notepad, Notepad++, VSCode, Atom, PyCharm. Any violation of this will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Participants found downloading entire templates will be disqualified. But participants are permitted to use the internet (for small blocks of code to implement a certain functionality) to aide them in building the webpage/app.
  • The participants will have to heed to the instructions given by the CodeFury 3.0 organising committee and the CodeFury 3.0 volunteering committee.
  • The final winner will be chosen based on their presentation and the innovation/idea behind their webpage or app.
  • The final decision is based on the sole discretion of the panel of judges.
  • The decision made by the panel of judges is final and will not be amended under any circumstances.
  • Incase of any disputes, IEEE UVCE's decision is final.
